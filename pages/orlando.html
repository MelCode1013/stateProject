Orlando

A place that is well renowned for everyone's favorite mouse!

Population

In 2020, Orlando is recorded to have had 307,573 people living in the urban city, making it the fourth-most populous city in Florida. Many people come from all over to visit the most renown attractions, Disney World and Universal Studios. Not to mention the nightlife, bars, and clubs in Downtown Orlando. It's no wonder so many people come to visit as well as call Orlando their home.

Orlando's Economy

Orlando is a huge industrial and tech center. The city is nationally recognized in its innovation in aerospace, aviation, and more. The low cost of doing business in Orlando is what attracts so many companies, which in turn, brings in more jobs.

Orlando's beginnings

In 1840, a small group of people grew up around Fort Gatlin, a fort built to protect settlers from Native American attacks during the Seminole war. The community was known as Jernigan, named after the Jernigan family. As the settlement expanded northward, the community changed its name to "Orlando".
