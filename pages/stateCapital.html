Tallahassee - FL state capital

Home of the FSU Seminoles

Population

Tallahassee is well-known as being a college town, with a student population that exceeds 70,000. The city's population is 201,731 as of 2022. That makes Tallahassee the eigth-most populated urban city in FL.

Tallahassee's Economy

Some of the top employers for the area includes:

  1. State of Florida
  2. Florida State University
  3. Tallahassee Memorial Hospital
  4. Leon County School Board

Tallahassee's history

Legislators had to do government business between Augustine and Pensacola. Unfortunately, the traveling was very dangerous and took 20 days! Tallahassee was founded as Florida's state capital, as it was the midway point between the two principal cities.
